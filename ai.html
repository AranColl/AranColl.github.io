AI PROJECTS

Some basic games, where I learned the use of the max-min algorithm and discrete mathematics, that use Artifical Intelligence capable to either help or beat you at games. Part of a larger online Cambridge course.



AI tictactoe

The famous tictactoe game played against an AI player that uses the max-min algorithm. Part of an AI course, you can see the full code and play agaist it. I challenge you to try to win!

AI minesweeper

The minesweeper puzzle game now with a bit of help of an AI. Also part of an AI course, the AI is able to correctly choose the safe cells to win the game.

Chair x Room Classifier

This project was developed as a challenge. 5 days and a maximum of 5 hours were given to make decisions on how to approach the problem and implement a solution. To show my skills I decided to go with the Greedy Best-First Search to classify each chair within the correct room.
