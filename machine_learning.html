ML PROJECTS

End-to-end Machine Learning projects. From data gathering and cleaning, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, model training and prediction, and finally, data visualization. All follow the same procedure despite the differences we might encounter in the needs of the project, such as supervised/unsupervised or text/geo analytic techniques.



Lead Generation and Churn

A lab practice where using a Logistic Regression model new potential customers are detected and those unsatisfied are identified, emphasising the importance of the cutoff value.

Customer Segmentation

By applying unsupervised learning techniques, in this lab practice we are able to cluster our customer base and generate business insights.

NLP for Customer Care

For this practice, we use text analytic methodologies to identify leaknesses, unsatisfied customers and potential issues from their comments on the products.

Geo-Visual Analytics

Build a predictive model for store revenues and help Starbucks on where it should open a new coffee shop in Long Island, NY, according to our geo-data.

FP: GeoAnalysis of Complaints in NY

In this final project, both suspects and victims are profiled and clustered among all complaints recorded in NY. Afterwards, the geo features of the data are used for a more in-depth analysis of the most dangerous areas in NY.
