About Me

My name is Aran Coll and I was born and raised in the city of Barcelona, Spain. I have most of the time lived with my parents and younger brother, which have always been people to look up to and very supportive with my life projects. Since very young, I have found maths and informatics fascinating and really appealing, and that's why I chose the technological option in high school, and two years later, I was applying to the Mathematical Engineering in Data Science Bachelor's Degree at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, in Barcelona. Throughout the degree, I did not stop learning new concepts and applying them to become a Data Scientist, however, the biggest growth came with my Exchange Program at Essex University, where I got to meet people with very different ideas and make friends from different cultures. Now, I have just finished my degree, and I am very excited about the journey I have in front of me, where luckily I will keep learning and meeting marvelous people. As for my hobbies and my character, I love sports. I played roller hockey for most of my childhood and adolescence, I have recently taken up tennis, and I also enjoy watching football matches. I love animals. Some years ago my family adopted a little puppy which put the house upside down and changed my life forever, being vegetarian since then. Chilling reading a book or meeting with friends and talk through literally any topic would make my day.
