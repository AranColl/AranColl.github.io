END OF DEGREE PROJECT

My end of degree project was the implementation of an agent-based model of the city of Barcelona (Spain) resembling the COVID-19 pandemic era to evaluate the efficiency of the health measures applied to combat it. This project was done under the supervision and help of IPER and reached a very basic level of real detail to be used for any decision-making process. However, and after the delivery, I have kept working to merge my project together with my collegues' ones, focused on the mobility and behaviour of the population. Please feel free to add any comments on either resources or get in touch with me for any doubt or discussion that might come up.
